    Target audience

    The target audience of my job connecting website is people who are looking for remote work opportunities in the tech industry. They are mostly young professionals who have skills in web development, design, marketing or data analysis. They live in different countries and regions, but they share a common desire to work flexibly and independently. They are also interested in learning new skills and growing their careers. They use online platforms and social media to search for jobs, network with others and showcase their portfolios. By understanding my target audience, I can create a website that matches them with suitable employers and projects, and provides them with resources and support to succeed in their remote work.
